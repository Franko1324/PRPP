            <h1>Erasmus+</h1>
            <p>ERASMUS+ is the largest program of the European Union for education, training, youth and sports. It includes all European and international programs and initiatives of the European Union in the field of education (general education, higher education, adult education), training (vocational education and training), youth and sports.</p>
            <p>The Erasmus+ program offers TVZ students financial support for studying for one semester during their studies at partner higher education institutions within the European Union or financial support for an internship in a company within the European Union for two to six months. The competition for Erasmus+ mobility is usually announced twice a year - at the beginning of each semester.</p>
            <p>Since 2015, within the framework of the Erasmus+ program (KA171), academic exchange opportunities have been opened for students and staff with institutions of higher education from the so-called partner countries, i.e., countries outside the EU. As in the case of cooperation with program countries, cooperation with partner countries is based on inter-institutional agreements.</p>
